SOME OF MY FAV FOODS


BURGER

Burger comes in a very big variety of tastes depending on ingredients, making, the size and texture etc. On their own, they taste pretty good.

BROWNIE WITH ICE-CREAM

The luscious, creamy chocolate brownie is amazing served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, raspberry sauce and a sprinkling of toasted hazelnuts.

PIZZA

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.
